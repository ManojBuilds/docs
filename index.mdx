# Send Your Form to Google Sheets, Instantly

**Heysheet** provides a powerful and simple **form backend for Google Sheets**. It's the fastest way for developers to collect form submissions and send them directly to Google Sheets or Notion with zero backend setup. Our **Google Sheets form API** is designed for simplicity and speed, letting you connect your frontend forms to a spreadsheet in minutes.

Whether you're working with a **React form to Google Sheets**, a simple **HTML form to Google Sheets**, or any other stack, Heysheet ensures your data is captured reliably.

## Why Heysheet is the Best Form Backend

- **Direct Google Sheets Integration**: Instantly sync form data to any connected spreadsheet.
- **No Backend Required**: Use our **form submission API endpoint** from your frontend, and we handle the rest.
- **Use Any Frontend**: Works with React, Vue, Svelte, or plain HTML to build your form.
- **Affordable Alternative**: A **cheap alternative to SheetMonkey and Formspree** with a generous free tier.
- **File Uploads**: Allow users to upload files through your forms.
- **Notifications**: Get instant Slack and email alerts for new submissions.
- **Secure API**: Every form has a unique, secure endpoint with spam protection.
- **Analytics**: Track form performance with our built-in analytics.

> ⚠️ Heysheet does **not store your form responses**. All submissions go directly to your Google Sheet, giving you full control over your data.
