# Welcome to Heysheet

**Heysheet** is the fastest way for developers to collect form submissions and send them directly to Google Sheets — with zero backend setup.

Designed with simplicity and speed in mind, Heysheet lets you wire up frontend forms to Google Sheets in minutes using a secure API endpoint.

Whether you're launching a marketing site, collecting leads, or building internal tools, Heysheet ensures your data lands instantly in your connected Sheet.

## Why developers use Heysheet

- **Google Sheets Integration** — Instantly sync form data to any connected spreadsheet.
- **No Backend Required** — Just call our API from your frontend — we handle the rest.
- **Custom Frontends** — Use any tech stack (React, Vue, plain HTML, etc.) to build your form UI.
- **Webhook-style Workflow** — Connect Heysheet to your Sheet like a serverless form handler.
- **File Uploads** — Accept and store user-uploaded files (paid plans).
- **Slack & Email Alerts** — Get notified instantly on new form submissions (paid plans).
- **Secure API** — Each form has a unique, secure endpoint with spam protection.
- **Submission Analytics** — Visual insights to track submissions and user behavior.

> ⚠️ Heysheet does **not store form responses**. Submissions go directly to your linked Google Sheet for full control and transparency.
