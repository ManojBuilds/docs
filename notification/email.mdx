## 🔧 How to Enable Email Notifications

1. Go to the [Heysheet Dashboard](https://heysheet.in/dashboard)
2. Select your form
3. Navigate to the **Alert & Integrations** section
4. Enter the email address where you want to receive alerts
5. Enable **Gmail notification**
6. Click **Save Settings**

## ✉️ Email Format

Every notification email includes:

- ✅ The form name
- ⏱ Timestamp of the submission
- 📝 All submitted fields and their values

## 💡 Tips

- Use a valid email address that you actively check.
- Emails are sent from `notify@mail.heysheet.in`. Whitelist this to prevent missing alerts.
- To **disable notifications**, just clear the email field and hit **Save**.

---

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

Not receiving email notifications?

- ✅ Make sure the correct email is entered
- 📥 Check your **Spam**, **Promotions**, or **Updates** tab
- 📤 Make sure your inbox isn't full or filtered
- ❓ Still stuck? Email us at [support@heysheet.in](mailto:support@heysheet.in)
