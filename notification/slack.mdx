## ‚öôÔ∏è How to Enable Slack Notifications

1. Go to the [Heysheet Dashboard](https://heysheet.in/dashboard)
2. Select the form you want to connect
3. Navigate to the **Alert & Integrations** section
4. Click on **Connect Slack**
5. Approve the Slack app and select the channel where notifications should be sent
6. Click **Save Changes**
