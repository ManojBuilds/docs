Heysheet offers flexible pricing designed to grow with you — whether you're a solo builder or scaling a business.

---

## 📦 Available Plans

| Plan   | Price (Monthly) | Best For                |
|--------|------------------|--------------------------|
| Free   | $0               | Getting started, testing ideas |
| Starter | $8/mo           | Growing businesses, creators   |

---

## 🆓 Free Plan

Perfect for individuals who are just getting started.

**Includes:**
- ✅ 1 Form
- ✅ 100 Submissions/month
- ✅ Drag & Drop Builder
- ✅ Google Sheets Sync
- ✅ Notion Integration

---

## ⭐ Starter Plan (Most Popular)

Ideal for creators, small businesses, and startups ready to grow.

**Includes:**
- ✅ Unlimited Forms
- ✅ 25,000 Submissions/month
- ✅ Drag & Drop Builder (Coming soon)
- ✅ Google Sheets Sync
- ✅ Notion Integration
- ✅ File Uploads
- ✅ Slack & Email Alerts
- ✅ Basic Analytics
- ✅ Automatic Redirect
- ✅ Domain Whitelisting
